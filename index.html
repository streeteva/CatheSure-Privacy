Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Our app allows patients to upload photos of their wounds securely to our server. These photos are collected by authorized nurses for AI training to improve medical diagnostics.

Information We Collect

  • Photos: Wound images uploaded by patients.
  • User Input: Labels indicating infection status and image type.
  • User ID: Used to associate photos with patient accounts.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide app functionality and to train AI models that assist medical professionals.

Data Security

We store your data securely on our servers. Only authorized medical staff and research teams have access to this data.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except authorized medical personnel involved in your care or research.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data by contacting our support team.

Contact Us
